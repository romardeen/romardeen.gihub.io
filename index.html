About Me

Research Interests: language documentation, sign language emergence, iconicity.

Education: BA Swarthmore College, MA Radboud University, PhD (ongoing) Uni Göttingen

Projects

Providence Island Sign Language

My work focuses on a small sign language used in the Caribbean island of Providence .

Perceived Iconicity in Foreign Signs

Previously, I have worked on how iconicity is perceived in foreign sign languages by deaf signers (a topic one cat describes as "astonishing").

South Rupununi Sign Language

I also have worked with Ben Braithwaite and Lily Kwok investigating a sign lanugage in the interior of Guyana.
